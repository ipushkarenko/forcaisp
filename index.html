About Me

Hi, I'm Yurii Pushkarenko. I'm a researcher focused on neural network architectures, deep learning, and computer vision.

I enjoy designing intelligent systems and exploring ways to improve how machines perceive and understand visual data. I also manage research groups working in the areas of 3D reconstruction and deep learning. My work lies at the intersection of academic rigor and practical software development.

I'm currently the principal engineer and head of Quality Engineering at the Center of Excellence at Intellias, where I apply advanced AI methods to solve real-world challenges.

I also lecture on Machine Learning, Data Mining, and Formal Methods at Taras Shevchenko National University of Kyiv.
